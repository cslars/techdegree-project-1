Fondue — Swiss Hot Pot

Fondue is a Swiss cheese dish. Cheese, chocolate, or other ingredients are melted and served as a communal dipping sauce, typically with bread, fruits, or other items.

A list of the ingredients needed to make a fondue:

  • 200 g of Gruyere cheese
  • 200 g of Emmental cheese
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 300ml of dry white wine
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • A pinch of white pepper
  • 1 French baguette or cubed bread for dipping

If you want to make your own fondue check out this recipe.
